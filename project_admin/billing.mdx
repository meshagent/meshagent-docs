Billing tools help you track usage and keep your project running without interruptions. Most teams configure billing in [MeshAgent Studio](../interfaces/meshagent_studio) before they deploy production workloads.

## What you can manage

- **Credits and balance** for your project.
- **Auto-reload** to prevent outages when credits run low.
- **Usage insights** by provider, room activity, and tool usage.

## Recommended practices

- Enable auto-reload for production environments.
- Review usage trends regularly when rolling out new agents.
- Separate environments (dev, staging, prod) to isolate spend.
